FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have a question? We've got answers. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to call us.

Is an appointment required to visit the clinic?
Yes, we recommend booking an appointment to minimise your waiting time and ensure a dentist is available to give you their full attention. You can book online, by phone, or via WhatsApp.
Do you accept walk-in patients?
We do accept walk-ins where possible, however availability cannot be guaranteed without an appointment. For urgent dental needs, please call us directly on +356 9969 8738 and we'll do our best to accommodate you.
What treatments and services do you offer?
We offer a comprehensive range of dental services including general dentistry, professional teeth cleaning, teeth whitening, root canal treatment, dental implants, and cosmetic dentistry. Our team is experienced in both preventive and restorative care.
How long does a typical dental visit take?
A routine check-up or cleaning usually takes between 30 to 60 minutes. More complex procedures such as root canals or implant consultations may take longer. We'll always give you a clear time estimate when you book your appointment.
Is dental treatment painful at GUMS Clinic?
We prioritise your comfort at every step. We use modern anaesthetic techniques and sedation options where needed to ensure procedures are as comfortable and painless as possible. Many of our patients are pleasantly surprised at how relaxed they feel.
How can I prepare for my first visit?
For your first visit, simply arrive a few minutes early. It helps to bring any previous dental records or X-rays if you have them. Let us know of any medical conditions or medications you're taking, so we can tailor your care appropriately.
